Single storey detached family house design with four rooms and an underground parking space for two cars and boiler room.

It’s main feature is a green roof garden that can be accessed both from the inside and outside staircases, and helps to control the house’s temperature in a natural way.

It has been designed with ample interior space and high ceilings in the living area, making possible a mezzanine library floor with an exit to the upper garden.

The exterior has two different entertainment areas, including a swimming pool.
